About DSA

WELCOME TO DEPTFORD SOCCER
WE ARE AN OPEN CLUB! Athletes from all townships are welcome.
The Deptford Soccer Association welcomes you to our site. Our Club is based out of Deptford New Jersey, which is located in Gloucester County. We are a non-profit and educational program whose mission is to foster the physical, mental and emotional growth and development of our youth through the sport of soccer at all levels of age and competition.
